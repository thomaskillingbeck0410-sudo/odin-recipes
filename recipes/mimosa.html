Mimosa

Description

This recipe found here, makes a deliciously simple cocktail with only two ingredients.

For more cocktail recipes visit the Recipes Index

Ingredients

This recipe makes 2 mimosas.

  • Orange Juice: 1/4 cup of chilled orange juice.
  • Champagne: 3/4 cup of chilled champagne or sparkling wine.

Steps

All you need is a cup.

  1. Split the orange juice between two glasses.
  2. Top with champagne.
  3. Enjoy!
