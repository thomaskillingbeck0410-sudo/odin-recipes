Bloody Mary

Description

A Bloody Mary is simple to make and produces a rich, spicy and savory cocktail. This recipe, found here, produces a Classic Bloody Mary to enjoy at your next get together.

For more cocktail recipes visit the Recipes Index

Ingredients

A Bloody Mary, as stated earlier, is fairly simple to make and only requires a handful of ingredients. These include:

  • Tomato Juice: A spicy, or non-spicy, tomato juice cocktail like V8 works best.
  • Vodka: This is the alcohol used in Bloody Marys, and stubstitutions may not produce the same taste.
  • Worcestershire Sauce: An important ingredient to add the salty and umami-rich flavor of a Bloody Mary.
  • Hot Pepper Sauce: Optional if you don't like spicy things. But great for adding that kick to your cocktail.
  • Salt and Pepper: This adds extra flavor.
  • Garnishes: A Bloody Mary usually has a number of garnishes. In this case, you can use celery and garlic-stuffed olives. However, garnishes can be substituted, added, or removed to your liking.

Simple, right? With only six ingredients it's an easy task to actually make the cocktail as well.

Steps

Since a Bloody Mary does not contain any carbonated beverages, all you need is a cocktail shaker.

  1. Put all the ingredients, except garnishes, into your cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake it up.
  3. Serve in your glass of choice with the chosen garnishes.
  4. Enjoy!!
