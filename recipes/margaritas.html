Frozen Margaritas

Description

This recipe, found here, puts a twist on the classic margarita with the use of frozen limemade and familiar margarita ingredients in order to make a refreshing summer cocktail.

For more cocktail recipes visit the Recipes Index

Ingredients

This recipe makes 4 margaritas:

  • Ice: 4 cups.
  • Frozen Limeade Concentrate: 1 can.
  • Tequila: 6 fluid ounces of tequila.
  • Triple Sec: 2 fluid ounces of triple sec.
  • Lime: Lime wedges for garnish.

Steps

Get out your blender and get mixing.

  1. Add your ingredients minus the lime wedges into a blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Serve in glasses with lime wedge garnishes.
  4. Enjoy!
