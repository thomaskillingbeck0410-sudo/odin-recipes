Frozen Margaritas

Description

This recipe, found here, puts a twist on the classic margarita with the use of frozen limemade and familiar margarita ingredients in order to make a refreshing summer cocktail.

For more cocktail recipes visit the Recipes Index

Ingredients

I'm tired of repeating myself; cocktails are easy to make and have like five ingredients in all of them. I might stop including a paragraph after ingredients entirely. How do recipe bloggers come up with so much to say.

  • Ice: This recipe makes four margaritas and calls for four cups. Add however much you want.
  • Frozen Limeade Concentrate: You'll need 1 can of this stuff.
  • Tequila: This recipe calls for 6 fluid ounces of tequila.
  • Triple Sec: You'll need 2 fluid ounces of triple sec.
  • Lime: You'll need some lime wedges for garnish.

Steps

Get out your blender and get mixing.

  1. Add your ingredients minus the lime wedges into a blender.
  2. Blend.
  3. Serve in glasses with lime wedge garnishes.
  4. Enjoy!
